Life Path 7 Compatibility


Life path number 7 embodies individuals who enjoy their birthdays on the 7th, 16th, or 25th of any month. Life path number 7 has been found to be compatible with life path 5 and 3, the reason being that 5 is a highly intellectual number, thus good for offering challenges and three is a generous and giving soul.

Number 7 is quite a unique number and any multi-digit number that will total to 7 after reducing to a single unit is a lucky number for the 7’s. Some of the lucky numbers are 7, 16, 25, and 34, other multi-digit numbers are such as 106 and 115 going forward.

Characteristics of the life path number 7 individuals

Privacy and solitude- life path number 7 individuals love their space, a fact that does not only apply to friends but even to the closest family member like their wives or husbands. When situations get out of hand, life path 7 individuals would prefer to work things out on their own, their determination and strong will always yield results.

Life path 7 individuals don’t go by the set society standards, rather they have their own devised methods, beliefs, and principles, that makes them explorers, mystic’s, and spiritual beings. The 7’s relate peace with being alone; they are, however, full of surprises when it comes to their personality, as they love self-discovery and despite their high demand for privacy they do have an attractive personality thus possess a magnetic charm.

They also like to boast and you will find them showing off their skills, their sense of humor is ripe, and love to spend time with their dear ones. The life path 7 individuals will spend time with people, have fun, but getting their alone time is like a pill that must be swallowed after being with people, which we must say is quite therapeutic, as it provides the opportunity to reflect on things, analyze and evaluate situations, and finally come up with solutions for the various issues.

And given that they rarely entertain people in their personal space, the 7’s tend to be even more protective of their inner self; you can also expect them to have a hard time trusting people, and are always skeptical about almost everything. 7’s are also famous for blowing things out of proportion, and will, therefore, become very suspicious of things around them most of which don’t have any element of truth.

These individuals have been created in such a way that they can out of nowhere become gloom-ridden, and somehow selfish, a thing that puts a strain on their relationship creating some form of distance between them and other people. And for as much as the 7’s love their solitary lives; they are also envious of socially active people.

They, however, have a hard time connecting with other people a thing that they don’t try so hard to do. And the best thing about them is that they have the ability to attain wisdom and knowledge, and which they do relentlessly, 7’s are knowledgeable thus good at analyzing situations in a sensible manner, which then helps them grow, nurture and evolve, in a way that makes them gain a deeper understanding about spirituality and humanity.

Love and relationships– 7’s are very sensitive in nature, and that is why some of them prefer to lead an unmarried life. For those in marriage, they tend to be very serious in making it work, they do possess the real true love, and the love is all-round especially for their children, interestingly is that they don’t feel the same love for the children that aren’t theirs.

7’s are in for the long term when it comes to marriage and they will make one envious, given their ability to present very successful marriages, and a tight family unit. They have a small circle of friends who get the best of them and will most of the time pair up with people who can tackle difficult situations. They are also not very good at saving money and love to tour other countries.

When they find compatible partners, life path 7’s can make a good family life and home, but the opposite will most of the time result in divorce.

Jobs and professions– The life path 7 individuals love jobs that allow them to make independent decisions, and will also do jobs that offer them an opportunity to tour other foreign countries. They love to live a happy and prosperous life but will not tolerate being dictated upon or work under undue pressure, more so professionally.

These individuals don’t attract good luck, if you, therefore, find one, or you are one of them, you can expect to enjoy success after putting in a lot of effort. They always struggle with recognition and acceptance, failure is part of their life’s journey and you will find them doing some of the most unfulfilling jobs, and despite their hard work, will power and intelligence, they tend to have a hard time getting to the place that their career should take them.

Life path 5 characteristics

The 5’s have been determined to make good life partners with the 7’s, and we are curious to find out the nature of these individuals because from the above excerpt the 7’s are not the easiest to deal with. So one of the characteristics of the 5’s is that they love adventure a thing that is very similar to the 7’s given their love for travel to foreign countries; 5’s are the progressive thinking type of people, have the best communication skills which is also a good quality for the poor 7 communicators.

The 5’s love their freedom and are better placed to understand and offer the same to the 7’s. They love to multi-task, and will, therefore, explore different areas of life, they easily adapt to change, they are not foolish and will, therefore, dig for information wherever they go or settle. Given their aggressive nature, the 5’s love to work in fields that are engaging and friendly, they love to communicate and don’t have a problem engaging with people. They are pretty confident about themselves and don’t have a problem even talking in public.

Interestingly when it comes to love and relationships the life path 5 individuals refrain from getting into committing relationships. However, they recognize beauty, and love to explore sensuality, food, love, and sex. They know where to get full life enjoyment and will, therefore, engage in the sensory pleasures, important to note is that if they decide to get into a commitment, they become the most loyal and faithful partners, and do their best to make the relationship work.

Life path 3 characteristics

The life path 3 and 7 individuals are compatible at certain levels and there are also chances for their relationship to hit the rocks as soon as it begins. What is surprising about the life path 3 person is that they tend to possess most of the characteristics of the life path 7 individuals except for the fact that the 7’s easily retreat to seclusion.

Individuals under life path 3 are very playful, independent have good communication skills which could help with the 7’s. The 3’s have creative energy and are multi-talented, and you will, therefore, find them in various fields such as Counseling, singing, writing, or they may work as radio broadcasters.

So, one of the reasons as to why they might work out with the 7’s is because the 3’s are extremely generous and giving souls, and will only derive positivity in everything around them. They are easy to love and be around, simply because they have a charismatic personality, are good listeners, and highly conscious of other people’s feelings and emotions.

The above then means that the 3’s are well capable of making the people around them feel comfortable, and this also goes for respecting their privacy. 3’s care less about tomorrow, they live for the moment and believe that tomorrow will take care of itself, a character that wouldn’t rub nicely on 7.

They are not the most responsible people as they love to procrastinate, and rarely have direction in their life; they are also very emotional and will lock people out when hurt. Bitterness is in their system more so when hurt and can, therefore, make very hurtful comments.
